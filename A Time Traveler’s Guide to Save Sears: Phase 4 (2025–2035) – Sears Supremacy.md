## The Sears Concordant (aka: The Syndicate)

A unified alliance between an industrialized **Sears** and its industrial suppliers, employees, customers and each other.
Bound by a covenant of reciprocity, durability and repair.

### Mission
To Cement **Sears** as a vertically integrated, AI, crypto-powered retail-industrial mega-corporation, valued at **$3 trillion** by 2035, through Sears.com, generating **$900 billion** in revenue.

* Offer **102M+** stock-keeping units (SKUs).
* Serve **350M** users.
* Command a **35%** share of the North American market, capturing **$1.4T** out of a $4T total e-commerce market.
* Secure a **13%** share in expanded markets (Japan, Taiwan, Indonesia, Australia/New Zealand, Singapore, Philippines, Vietnam, Argentina, UK/Ireland), capturing **$585B** out of a $4.5T Southeast Asia and emerging regions market.
* Total E-commerce Revenue (2035): **$1.985T** ($1.4T NA + $585B Expanded Markets)
* Maintain working class hero reputation.
* Continue developing "safe harbor" ecosystem.

    * **2035 Target:** $23.2B
    * **Holdings (2035):** Precious Metals Mining Securities ($18B), Bitcoin Reserve ($5.2B).

    * **Purpose:** Hold 7% of Sears equity for dedicated long-term investors
    * **2025 Assets:** $70B (7% of $1T valuation)
    * **2035 Assets:** $210B (7% of $3T valuation)
    * **Board Seats:** 2 reserved for Sears Fund representation on main Sears board.

4.  **Homart Community Fund**
    * **Purpose:** Finance community initiatives at Homart malls
    * **2025 Assets:** $7B
    * **2035 Assets:** $10B
    * **Annual Disbursements:** ~$500M/year for community programs.

5.  **Sears Supplier Growth Fund (SSGF)**
    * **Purpose:** Collective Syndicate supplier investment in Sears equity; defensive position against hostile takeovers
    * **Structure:** Suppliers contribute from profit-sharing (10% of distributions), Sears matches 50% (up to $500M/year cap).
    * **2035 Assets:** $23B (0.77% of Sears)

6.  **Employee Stock Purchase Program (ESPP)**
    * **Purpose:** Employee ownership with Class A voting shares (10 votes each)
    * **Structure:** Employees contribute up to 15% of salary, Sears matches 50% (up to 7.5% of salary), 15% discount.
    * **2035 Assets:** $36B (1.2% equity = 12% voting power)

7.  **Sears Ventures**
    * **Purpose:** Venture capital for SMBs, startups aligned with Sears ecosystem
    * **2025 Assets:** $20B
    * **2035 Assets:** $30B
    * **Focus Areas:** NA SMB, Gaming, Electric/hybrid vehicles, etc.

8.  **Customer Brokerage Accounts**
    * **2035 Assets:** $80B
    * **Management:** Standard fiduciary oversight.

9.  **Sears Savings Accounts**
    * **Revenue (2035):** $0.6B
    * **Features:** Integrated with **Dean Witter** banking services.
